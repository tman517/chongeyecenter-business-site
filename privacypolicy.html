Text Message Privacy Policy (簡訊隱私權政策)

Chong Eye Center, PLLC is committed to safeguarding the privacy of our patients. We want to assure you that we do not share your personal information with third parties. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us.

Chong Eye Center, PLLC 致力於保護病患的隱私。我們想向您保證，我們不會與第三方分享您的個人資訊。本隱私權政策概述了我們如何收集、使用和保護您提供給我們的資訊。


Information Collection (資訊收集): 

We collect only the information necessary to contact you and to provide the services that you request from us. This may include name, phone number, etc. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with any third parties. 

我們僅收集聯繫您和提供服務所需的信息 您向我們提出的要求。這可能包括姓名、電話號碼等。 出租或與任何第三方分享這些資訊。


How We Use Your Information (我們如何使用您的訊息): 

The information collected is used solely for scheduling and confirming appointments. We do not share your information with external parties for marketing or any other purposes. 

收集的資訊僅用於安排和確認預約。我們不會出於行銷或任何其他目的與外部各方分享您的資訊。


Your Choices (您的選擇): 

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. If you have any concerns or questions about your data, please contact us at chongeyecenter@gmail.com. 

您有權存取、更正或刪除您的資訊。如果您對自己的資料有任何疑慮或疑問，請透過 chongeyecenter@gmail.com 與我們聯繫。


Policy Changes (政策變化): 

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you, and your continued use of our services implies your acceptance of the updated policy. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. 

我們可能會不時更新我們的隱私權政策。任何變更都會傳達給您，您繼續使用我們的服務即表示您接受更新後的政策。使用我們的服務即表示您同意本隱私權政策中所列的條款。
