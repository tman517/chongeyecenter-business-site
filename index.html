Day Of Visit
If it is your first visit, plan to arrive 10 minutes prior to your appointment. You will be warmly greeted and we will assist you with some limited paperwork prior to your meeting with the doctor. Dr. Chong will perform a thorough eye exam and patiently discuss his findings and any concerns you may have. We recommend that new patients have their eyes dilated. Most visits will last approximately 1 hour.
ABOUT THE DOCTOR
James Chong, M.D. is a board-certified ophthalmologist. He grew up in Houston and graduated valedictorian from Memorial High School. Dr. Chong received his Bachelor of Arts degree in anthropology from Rice University and Doctor of Medicine from Baylor College of Medicine. He trained in ophthalmology at the University of Texas Medical School at Houston. Prior to starting Chong Eye Center, Dr. Chong worked in a group practice in Dallas.
Dr. Chong is dedicated to his patients and their vision. From routine eye exams to eye problems requiring surgery, Dr. Chong gives each patient their clearest vision with the highest levels of safety and comfort.
When not working, he enjoys spending time with his wife and two daughters.
