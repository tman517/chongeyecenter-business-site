Day Of Visit
Plan to arrive 10 minutes prior to your appointment to complete personal and medical information forms. We are a patient-focused clinic and your exams will be performed from start to finish by Dr. Chong. For new patients and yearly examinations, we recommend that you consider having your eyes dilated. The visit will typically last approximately an hour.
ABOUT THE DOCTOR
James Chong, M.D. is a board-certified ophthalmologist. He grew up in Houston and graduated valedictorian from Memorial High School. Dr. Chong received his Bachelor of Arts degree in anthropology from Rice University and Doctor of Medicine from Baylor College of Medicine. He trained in ophthalmology at the University of Texas Medical School at Houston. Prior to starting his own clinic, Dr. Chong worked in a group practice in Dallas.
Dr. Chong is dedicated to his patients and their vision. From routine eye exams to eye problems requiring surgery, Dr. Chong gives each patient their clearest vision with the highest levels of safety and comfort.
When not working, he spends time with his wife and daughter. The family recently took a trip to Disney World!
